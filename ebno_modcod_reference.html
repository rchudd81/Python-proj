2. Link Budget Analysis Formulas

Spectral Efficiency = m × FEC
Eb/No = Effective C/N - 10 × log10(m × FEC)
Dish 2 Pre-jam C/N = Dish 1 Pre-jam C/N + 20 × log10(Dish2_RX / Dish1_RX) + (BeamGain2 - BeamGain1)
Effective C/N (jam impact model) = Pre-jam C/N - JammerRise_dB
Post-jam SNR display = Pre-jam C/N + JammerRise_dB
Required Eb/No for stable lock = Assigned Min Eb/No + 2.0 dB
Lock condition: Eb/No >= Required Eb/No
Dish 2 jammer rise at lock loss = Dish2_EbNo(no-jam) - Required Eb/No

3. How the Tool Decides Lock

Condition Result
Dish 1 Eb/No and Dish 2 Eb/No both >= required threshold Green tile (lock probable)
Either dish falls below required threshold Red tile (no lock)
